A beautiful flirt, the Medusa is aligned with the Villagers but harbours a terrifying secret. During the day they are well known in the village of Ferns for their stunning appearance which captures the eye and love of all the townsfolk. However when their secret takes ahold at sundown, their true self is revealed. Any who gaze upon their true form would see live snakes for hair and the few that further look into their eyes are turned to stone.
